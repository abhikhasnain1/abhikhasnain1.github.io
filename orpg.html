Ninith

Project Description

Ninith is a solo, print-and-play RPG designed for replayable sci-fi adventures. A pencil, an eraser, a D6 and a D20 dice are all you need. Play as Ninith, a biomechanoid escaping their home planet. The galaxy is rich with encounters, entities, planets and resources. Survival is neither guaranteed nor easy. Choose from 4 core and 6 special abilities. Navigate your way out across 10 sectors. Balance resources, protect your hull and try your best not to run out of fuel. Hopefully you stand a chance.

Features

  • Navigate a 6×12 grid with five possible directions each turn.
  • Balance scrap, materials, fuel and hull to survive and increase your final score.
  • 20 entities, 10 abilities, 6 planets and 6 materials combine to create new narrative setups each run.
  • Encounters unfold through 16 possible outcomes based on player and entity stance.
  • Hand-drawn digital art by Abhik Hasnain.
  • Interfaces for tracking all stats and progress are provided on the sheet.
